# FTC Team 22689
## Connections
### Control Hub
- Motor Port 0: Front Left Motor
- Motor Port 1: Front Right Motor
- Motor Port 2: Back Left Motor
- Motor Port 3: Back Right Motor
- Servo Port 0: Launch Servo
- I2C0: (IMU)
- I2C1: Pinpoint Computer
- RS485: Expansion Hub
### Expansion Hub
- Motor Port 0: Outtake Motor (Encoder)
- Motor Port 1: Intake Motor
- Motor Port 2: Lift Left Motor (Encoder)
- Motor Port 3: Lift Right Motor (Encoder)
- RS485: Control Hub
## Controls
### Driver 1
- Left Stick Y-Axis: Forward/Backward Movement
- Left Stick X-Axis: Left/Right Strafing
- Right Stick X-Axis: Rotation
- Left Trigger: Slow left Strafe
- Right Trigger: Slow right Strafe
### Driver 2
- DPad Up: Lift Up
- DPad Down: Lift Down
- DPad Left: Intake In
- Dpad Left: Intake Out
